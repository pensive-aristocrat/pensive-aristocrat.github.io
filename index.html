About

I am a Postbac Research Assistant at the Center for Astronomy, Space Science and Astrophysics, Independent University, Bangladesh. My work focuses on the modeling of strong gravitational lensing systems and the development of computational tools for the astronomical community.

I am one of the co-developers of dolphin , an AI-powered automated pipeline for lens modeling, using lenstronomy as the modeling engine.
